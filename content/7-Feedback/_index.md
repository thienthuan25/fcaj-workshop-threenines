### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**

The working environment at FCAJ is truly excellent. The workspace is professional, well-organized, and comfortable, making it easy to stay focused and work efficiently. Everyone is friendly and approachable, which helped me quickly integrate into the team even as a new intern. It is an ideal environment for a student like me to learn and grow.

**2. Support from the Mentor and Team Admin**

My mentor and the team admins provided tremendous support throughout my internship. Whenever I encountered difficulties or had questions, they were always willing to provide clear and thoughtful guidance. My mentor not only helped me with technical challenges but also encouraged me to research and solve problems independently before offering hints, which greatly improved my critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

**3. Relevance of the Internship to My Major**

I am majoring in Computer Science, and my long-term career goal is to become a Cloud Engineer. The work I performed during my internship at FCAJ aligned perfectly with this career path. I had the opportunity to work directly with AWS services and Infrastructure as Code tools such as Terraform, which strengthened both my technical foundation and my practical experience for my future career.

**4. Learning and Skill Development Opportunities**

During the internship, I learned many valuable lessons, especially through the events organized by the program. These events not only expanded my technical knowledge but also broadened my perspective on the cloud computing industry and helped me develop a clearer vision for my future career.

**5. Culture and Team Spirit**

The culture at FCAJ is very positive. Everyone respects one another and works professionally while maintaining a friendly and welcoming atmosphere. Whenever teamwork is required, people are always willing to support each other regardless of their roles or positions. This made me feel that I was truly part of the team.

**6. Internship Policies and Benefits**

The internship program provides excellent support for interns through flexible working hours and numerous training sessions and internal knowledge-sharing events. Having the opportunity to experience a real office environment was also a valuable benefit that motivated me and gave me a more professional working experience.

### Personal Reflections

The aspect I appreciated the most was witnessing the professionalism and attention to detail in the way people worked at the office. The professional working culture inspired me to study harder and motivated me to pursue greater career achievements in the future.

I believe the company could improve the office registration process. Since there are many interns, scheduling office visits for everyone each week can be challenging, resulting in limited opportunities for each intern to work on-site.

I have already recommended this internship program to my friends, and some of them joined after me. I truly believe this is a worthwhile program, especially for students who aspire to build a career in cloud computing.

### Suggestions and Expectations

Because there are many interns, each person's opportunity to register for office days is somewhat limited. I hope the program can provide additional office space so that more interns have the opportunity to work on-site more frequently.

I sincerely hope to continue being part of the program in the future because there is still so much I want to learn from **First Cloud AI Journey**. During the events, I especially enjoyed listening to the speakers share their knowledge and experiences. I hope that one day I will become one of those speakers myself, standing on stage to present and share my own experiences at future events.

Finally, I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the entire **First Cloud AI Journey** team for supporting and accompanying me throughout my internship. It has been a truly memorable journey that provided me with valuable knowledge, practical skills, and unforgettable experiences.
